TOP CHURN DRIVERS (PLAIN-LANGUAGE, SHAP-ALIGNED)
1) Customers in month-to-month contracts during their first year are the highest-risk group (early month-to-month cohort).
2) Fiber internet customers without online security churn much more often than the base population.
3) Customers paying by electronic check with paperless billing show a materially elevated churn pattern.
4) Customers with high monthly bills and short tenure churn at a much higher rate than average.
5) Contract structure remains a dominant signal: long-term contracts suppress churn risk, while month-to-month exposure increases it.

BUDGET USE GUIDANCE
- Use threshold 0.28 for production campaign targeting.
- Use $20 as the baseline intervention cost for the At Risk targeting workflow.
- Do not use the pure cost-optimized 0.11 threshold for production because precision drops too low.
